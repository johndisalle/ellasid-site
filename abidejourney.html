What It Does

Daily Verse Cards

A new scripture every day, beautifully formatted and ready to share to Instagram Stories or iMessage with a tap.

Couples Journal

Write privately, or share entries with your partner. Build a record of your faith journey together over time.

Couples Invite

Send a link to your partner via text or iMessage. When they tap it, the app opens and links your accounts automatically.

Apple Watch App

Glance at your verse of the day from your wrist. No phone needed for your morning moment with scripture.

Home Screen Widgets

Today's verse on your home screen — small, medium, and large sizes so scripture is always visible.

CloudKit Sync

Your journals and progress sync seamlessly across iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch via iCloud.
