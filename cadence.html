What It Does

One-Tap Logging

Create an event once. Every time you do it, tap the button. Cadence timestamps it and updates your next-due date automatically.

Smart Reminders

Set an interval — daily, weekly, monthly, or custom — and get a notification when you're overdue. No nagging, just a nudge at the right time.

Streaks & Insights

See your consistency over time. Rhythm Report gives you a picture of how reliably you're keeping cadence with the things that matter.

Apple Watch

Log events from your wrist. See what's coming up due without opening your phone. Works independently for quick-log moments.

Siri & App Intents

"Hey Siri, log oil change in Cadence." Shortcuts support lets you automate logging for things you do on a predictable schedule.

CloudKit Sync

Your events and logs sync across iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch instantly via iCloud. Start on one device, continue on another.
