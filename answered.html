The Story Behind It

Plaza Services LLC v. DiSalle · Eau Claire County · 2025SC000885

Built after a real pro se debt lawsuit.

Answered was created by John DiSalle after he represented himself in Eau Claire County Small Claims Court in a consumer debt collection case. The case, Plaza Services LLC v. DiSalle, was dismissed on April 9, 2026. That experience shaped Answered around the practical problem people face when court papers arrive: understanding the deadline, preparing an Answer, and taking the next step instead of defaulting.

What It Does

Answer Packet

Answered guides users through a self-help workflow for creating a filing-formatted Answer Packet where supported.

Deadline Orientation

The deadline checker helps users identify the general response window for their state and reminds them to verify the exact date with their court papers.

Plaintiff Pages

High-intent guides explain what to look for when sued by companies such as LVNV Funding, Midland Credit Management, and Portfolio Recovery Associates.

Debt Buyer Proof

Answered explains common proof issues in debt buyer cases, including chain of title, account ownership, and documentation gaps.

Full Defense Upgrade

Users who need more than an Answer Packet can upgrade into deeper defense organization, including discovery and advanced defense content.

Responsible Self-Help

Every page is written to make the limits clear: Answered is software, not a law firm, lawyer, or substitute for court-specific legal advice.
