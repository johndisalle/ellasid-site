The Story Behind It

Plaza Services LLC v. DiSalle · Eau Claire County · 2025SC000885

Built by someone who actually won.

The app's creator was personally sued for debt in Wisconsin — a 9-month pro se case against a debt buyer. He filed his own Answer, ran his own defense, and won. The case was dismissed without prejudice on April 9, 2026, after the court found the plaintiff failed to prove AAA compliance. Answered was built to give everyone else the same fighting chance.

What It Does

Answer Generator

Scan or enter your summons details. The app generates a proper legal Answer — the document you file with the court to avoid a default judgment.

Defense Playbook

A step-by-step guide through the small claims process — from filing your Answer through discovery, hearings, and potential dismissal.

Plaintiff Identification

Recognize your plaintiff instantly. Answered knows the most common debt buyers operating across all supported states and their typical weaknesses.

Weakness Analysis

Surface FDCPA violations, arbitration clause opportunities, statute of limitations issues, and AAA compliance failures — the exact levers that win cases.

Case Tracking

Log every hearing date, filing deadline, and communication. Your case timeline stays organized so nothing slips through the cracks.

PDF Export

Export your generated Answer as a clean PDF, ready to print and file at the courthouse. No formatting needed.
