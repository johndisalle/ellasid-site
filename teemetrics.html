What It Does

Live GPS Yardages

Stand over the ball and see front, center, and back distances to the green in real time. No manual input — just open the app and play.

661 Pre-Loaded Courses

Real GPS coordinates pulled from a pro course database — not approximations. If your course is in the list, the data is accurate.

Hazard Calculator

Pro feature: see distances to water, bunkers, and out-of-bounds from your current position. Take the guesswork out of club selection.

Hole-by-Hole Scoring

Log your score, putts, fairways hit, and GIR for every hole. Build a complete picture of where your game is breaking down.

Apple Watch App

Check yardages from your wrist mid-round without pulling out your phone. Glances, complications, and full app on watchOS 10.

Round History

Every round saved, every trend tracked. Compare your performance across courses and conditions over time. Know your game.
